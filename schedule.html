holiday schedule 2021

When it comes to the Holidays, it will only impact you if it falls directly on your pick up day. If the holiday does not fall on your pick up day then your schedule will stay the same that week of the holiday. If you do not understand, please call the office for further information in regards to the holidays. Thank you

November 25, 2021 (Thanksgiving Day)- Trash Pick Up will be on Monday, November 22, 2021 & Monday, November 29, 2021 (For recycle, please refer to recycle schedule under "Recycle" Tab.
We will be running regular routes on Friday, December 24, 2021 (Christmas Eve), but the office will be closed. TRASH WILL STILL BE PICKED UP ON THIS DAY.

IF YOUR TRASH OR RECYCLE IS NOT ON THE HOLIDAY YOU WILL NOT BE AFFECTED. ANY QUESTIONS IN REGARDS TO OUR HOLIDAY SCHEDULE PLEASE CONTACT OUR OFFICE. IF THIS SCHEDULE IS UNCLEAR TO YOU PLEASE DO NOT WAIT UNTIL THE WEEK OF THE HOLIDAY TO CALL THE OFFICE TO FIND OUT PICK UP DAY, CALL PRIOR TO THE WEEK OF HOLIDAY TO ENSURE YOU KNOW YOUR SCHEDULE AND ARE ABLE TO HAVE YOUR TRASH OUT ON THE RIGHT DAY.

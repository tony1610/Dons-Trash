Welcome To The Don's Trash Website

Don's Trash strives to provide optimal disposal services from your everyday household trash and recycling, to yard work and special pick up services. We provide many different service option combinations to suit your family, financial, and disposal needs! Our goal is to provide excellent customer and disposal services to our customers and their families. If you are interested in becoming a valued Don's Trash Customer, check out our service option combinations under our "Pricing" tab or just give us a call or stop by our office located on Ray Road. If you are already a Don's Trash customer checkout our quarterly, biannual, and annual rates located under the "Pricing" tab! Also checkout our "Guideline" and "Recycling" tabs for more information about our services to you. Whether you are a customer, wanting to become one, or just trying to inquire more information, call our office, we are open Monday through Friday, 8:30am till 3:30pm. Have a great day and God Bless, from all of us here at Don's Trash!
